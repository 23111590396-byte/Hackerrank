Claude by Anthropic — Privacy and Data Protection

## Privacy Overview

### Anthropic's commitment to privacy
Anthropic is committed to protecting user privacy. We handle personal data with care and transparency. Our privacy practices are governed by our Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

### Types of data collected

**Account data**: Name, email address, password (hashed), profile information.
**Conversation data**: The messages you send to Claude and Claude's responses.
**Usage data**: Feature usage, timestamps, session durations, model used.
**Technical data**: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies.
**Payment data**: For Pro subscribers, billing information is processed by our payment provider (Stripe) and not stored directly by Anthropic.

### How data is used
- To provide and improve the Claude service
- To train and improve AI models (subject to your preferences)
- To ensure safety and prevent abuse
- To communicate account and service information
- To comply with legal obligations

## User Rights

### Your rights under GDPR (EU/EEA users)
If you are in the EU or EEA, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):
- **Right to access**: Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Right to rectification**: Correct inaccurate data.
- **Right to erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Right to portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Right to object**: Object to processing for certain purposes.
- **Right to restrict processing**: Limit how your data is used.

To exercise GDPR rights, email: privacy@anthropic.com

### CCPA rights (California residents)
California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (Anthropic does not sell personal data), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising rights.

### Data deletion requests
Submit data deletion requests to privacy@anthropic.com. Include your registered email address. Processing takes up to 30 days. Conversations are deleted; account data is purged after account deletion.

### Opting out of AI training
API users: By default, data from API usage may not be used for training. Check the Anthropic API usage policy for current defaults. Submit requests to opt out to privacy@anthropic.com.
Claude.ai users: Contact privacy@anthropic.com to request that your conversations not be used for training.

## Data Security

### How is data protected?
Anthropic uses industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Access controls and audit logs
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- SOC 2 Type II compliance

### Data breach notification
In the event of a data breach affecting user data, Anthropic will notify affected users as required by applicable law. Notifications are sent via email to your registered address.

### Reporting security vulnerabilities
If you discover a security vulnerability in Claude or Anthropic's services, report it to security@anthropic.com. Do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities. Anthropic has a responsible disclosure policy.

## International Data Transfers

### Where is data stored?
User data is primarily stored in the United States. For EU/EEA users, data transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as required by GDPR.

### Cross-border transfers
Anthropic complies with applicable data transfer regulations. EU data transfer mechanisms are documented in the Privacy Policy.

## Cookies and Tracking

### Cookie usage
Claude.ai uses essential cookies for functionality, analytics cookies (with consent), and preference cookies. You can manage cookie preferences in the cookie consent banner or browser settings.

### Third-party services
Claude.ai uses third-party services including Stripe (payments), analytics providers, and infrastructure providers. These services have their own privacy policies.
